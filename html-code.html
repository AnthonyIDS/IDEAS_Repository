💡 AI Insights

AI can act as a creative partner for designing courses that are accessible, inclusive, and engaging. When aligned with the three UDL principles, AI supports flexibility from the start.

  • Representation: Offer content in multiple accessible formats.
    Example: Generate summaries, transcripts, or captions using tools like Copilot or ChatGPT.

⚠️ Important Information

Please review the following information carefully to ensure you understand course expectations, requirements, and available support resources.

  • Course Expectations: Stay actively engaged by reviewing course materials, participating in discussions, and completing assignments on time.
  • Communication: Regularly check Canvas announcements and your SPC email for important updates and instructor communication.
  • Accessibility & Support: SPC provides academic and accessibility support resources to help you succeed throughout the course.
  • Need Help? Reach out to your instructor or utilize SPC student support services whenever you have questions or need assistance.

How do Large Language Models primarily learn?

A. By memorizing a database of facts
Not quite. LLMs do not simply memorize facts.
B. By predicting the next word in text
Correct! Large Language Models learn by predicting the next word (or token) in a sequence of text.
C. By watching videos like humans do
Not quite. LLMs are primarily trained on text rather than learning like humans do.
D. By browsing the internet in real time
Not quite. Most LLMs do not browse the internet in real time by default.
